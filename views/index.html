This app will create an online store. It should be able to add/edit/delete products, signup and track customers, search or browse for products from a list and show individual products pages, allow customer to put items in a "cart" and make a purchase (imaginary for now).

It should be somewhat generic so that it could be customized and used by a variety of businesses.
